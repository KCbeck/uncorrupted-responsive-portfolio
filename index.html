About Me

Spending my childhood between Kansas City and various parts of Colorado I moved to Kearney Missouri when I was 12. I worked odd jobs including as a farm hand, a mechanic and washing dishes at the Rockfront Bar and Grill in Holt Missouri. I joined the Army National Guard when I was 17 and completed Basic Training during the summer of my Junior Year.

I was part of Trumans Field Artillery Unit in Independence Mo. I tried out for 19th SFG at the Rec. in Watkins Colorado. Despite being asked to join the unit I had no where to stay so I created a play to get released from National Guard and attempted to get on active duty. I recruited for the Wisconsin National Guard for a few months and was released to go Active Army where I inprocessesed at Fort Hood in 2005

I got injured not long after and spent the next two years trying to stay in the military. Shortly after the injury I had a song held by major labels for many weeks, when the song was not cut, I realized that maybe I had what it takes to get a record deal. I began talking to BrokenBowRecords who signed Craig Morgan and Jason Aldean, but was told that they couldn't sign me due to being under contract with the military. I was released from active duty on March 20, 2007 where I immediately left to be a DOD contractor in Iraq. I spent the next 32 1/2 months in Iraq and went from being a mechanic for the Marine Corps to being the youngest safety officer in Iraq and part of the Senior Leadership Team. During that time In Iraq I recorded my album "My Car and My Guitar" flying back and fourth to nashville to do all the lyrics. It has gotten lots of attention over the years.

In 2010 shortly after leaving Iraq I began having memory issues, where I couldn't remember my lyrics. So I have taken a long break from music. During that time I have worked numerous investigator, security, and law enforcement jobs.

In 2018 I released my newest song "Hicks From The Sticks" which has gotten a lot of plays on Spotify and Itunes.

In 2019 I heard about the UT Coding academy and I'm currently working on that certification. I look forward to making the next big app
